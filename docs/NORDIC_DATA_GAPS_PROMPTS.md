## Search Strategy Tips

### General Patterns
```
# For CO2/LCA data:
[project name] "kg CO2" OR "kgCO2e" OR "hiilijalanjälki" OR "klimaaftryk" OR "klimatpåverkan"

# For circularity percentages:
[project name] "%" AND ("återbruk" OR "genbrug" OR "ombruk" OR "kierrätys")

# For official reports:
filetype:pdf [project name] (rapport OR report OR raportti)

# For thesis/research:
site:aaltodoc.aalto.fi OR site:diva-portal.org OR site:ntnuopen.ntnu.no [project name]
```

